uTunes

Finished

uTunes ia project made for my software development class. My team proposed an intelligent, flexible, lightweight music player that can be used without advanced computer or music knowledge. Users could import their music libraries and associate custom tags with their songs (ie. Rock, Happy, Sad, Party). Users could then quickly add songs to the playing queue by selecting which tags they want to listen to.
